  • Data Analytics & Processing — building analytical pipelines, visualization, pattern detection, and reporting.

Programming Languages

Python Rust

Python is our primary language for data analysis, machine learning, and rapid prototyping. Rust is used for developing high-performance, memory-safe system components that require minimal latency and maximum reliability.

Machine Learning & Data Analysis

We apply a broad range of machine learning methods depending on the nature of the task:

  • Gradient Boosting: XGBoost, CatBoost, LightGBM — for classification and regression on tabular data.
  • Ensemble Methods: Random Forest, model stacking — for improved prediction stability and accuracy.
  • Neural Networks: fully connected architectures, 1D CNNs, recurrent networks (LSTM, GRU) — for working with sequences and time series.
  • Transformer-Based Models — for tasks requiring complex dependency modeling in data.
  • Unsupervised Learning: clustering, dimensionality reduction (PCA, t-SNE, UMAP) — for uncovering hidden structures in data.
  • Time Series Forecasting: building predictive models that account for seasonality, trends, and anomalies.

The selection of specific methods and models is based on a preliminary analysis of the client's data and the nature of the task at hand.

How We Work

Our process is designed to minimize your time investment and ensure full transparency at every stage.

  1. Initial Inquiry. You reach out to us. We send you a brief questionnaire to understand the nature of your task and your expectations.
  2. Analysis & Proposal. Based on the information provided, we conduct a preliminary analysis, estimate the scope of work, and prepare a proposal with timelines and costs.
  3. Agreement & Kick-Off. Once terms are agreed upon, we begin work. You receive regular progress updates throughout the project.
  4. Development & Testing. We follow an iterative development process with intermediate demos. Every stage includes thorough testing and quality control.
  5. Invoicing & Acceptance. We issue a weekly invoice via DocuSign. Payment of the invoice constitutes acceptance of the completed work for that period, in accordance with our Terms & Conditions.
  6. Delivery & Support. Upon project completion, we deliver the finished solution and provide technical support as needed.
